About

Kevin Hefley is a dedicated creative with experience in acting, writing, directing, editing and producing for video and audio productions. A graduate of St. Louis Community College with a degree in Mass Communications, with a focus in broadcast. He has written, directed, and starred in several self-produced short films, and has had significant experience in live broadcast working as an on-air personality for KCFV 89.5 The Wave. Developing his technical skills in both the radio booth as well as a working TV studio, FVTV. He completed an internship at the local TV station FCTV. Kevin has also developed on-air personality while working in theatre both on and off stage.
